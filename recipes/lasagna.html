Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Mince Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the reicipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
